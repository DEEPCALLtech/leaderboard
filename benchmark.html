Medical Specialties Evaluation

Comprehensive assessment of small LLMs (under 13B parameters) across 1400 medical questions spanning multiple specialties and clinical scenarios.

Key Features:

  • Specialty Coverage: Extensive evaluation across medical specialties
  • Question Categories: Knowledge, action, emergency, diagnostic, disease identification, and real-life scenarios
  • Performance Analytics: Model weight vs accuracy analysis and similarity networks
  • Clinical Focus: Stress-testing models on life-threatening situations

Evaluation Scope: 1400 questions manually graded by Dr. Yohann Missiak MD

Target Models: Small portable LLMs suitable for local deployment

Research Goal: Understanding reasoning patterns and domain-specific capabilities
