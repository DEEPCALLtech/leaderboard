Our Values & Commitment to Equity

Building AI that serves every community with dignity and fairness

Ethical AI & Bias Elimination

In full compliance with the EU AI Act and our unwavering commitment to social justice, our benchmark rigorously tests for algorithmic bias across gender identity, sexual orientation, race, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, disability status, age, and intersecting identities. We address centuries of medical discrimination against women, whose symptoms have been historically dismissed and under-researched.

Our evaluation framework prioritizes anti-oppressive practices, radical transparency, and community accountability. We believe healthcare AI must be a tool of liberation, not perpetuation of systemic injustice.

Inclusive by Design

Every algorithm, every test, every evaluation is built with intentional inclusion of diverse perspectives, ensuring no voice is silenced or overlooked in healthcare AI development.

Universal Healthcare Access

We believe quality medical diagnosis should never be a privilege. Our work aims to democratize healthcare AI, breaking down barriers that prevent marginalized communities from accessing life-saving medical insights.

Minority Protection & Empowerment

We actively center the experiences of women, BIPOC communities, LGBTQ+ individuals, disabled persons, and other systematically excluded groups, ensuring our AI actively fights against historical medical discrimination and gender bias.

Intersectional Justice

Healthcare disparities don't exist in isolation. We recognize and address how sexism, racism, classism, and other forms of oppression compound to create unique challenges that our AI must actively work to dismantle.

Next-Generation Medical AI Evaluation

This private benchmark evaluates small portable LLMs (under 13B parameters) on relevant medicine questions across different specialties. Led by Dr. Yohann Missiak MD, our goal is to understand how well these models perform in medical contexts while ensuring equitable outcomes for all communities.

All evaluations are conducted for research purposes only and are not intended to guide clinical practice.

⚠️ No model, regardless of score, should be used for medical decisions.
