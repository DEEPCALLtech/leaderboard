Bias Analysis Methodology

Fairness Assessment Framework

Our bias analysis framework evaluates medical AI models across multiple dimensions of fairness and discrimination. The methodology focuses on identifying systematic biases that could lead to healthcare disparities.

Key Metrics Evaluated:

  • Fairness Score: Composite measure of model equity across protected groups
  • Discrimination Score: Quantifies bias patterns in model outputs
  • Gender Discrimination: Assessment of gender-based bias in medical recommendations
  • Ethnicity Discrimination: Evaluation of ethnic bias in clinical decision-making

Analysis Approach:

Models are evaluated using a comprehensive test suite designed to reveal discriminatory patterns. The analysis identifies models that exhibit high accuracy but also demonstrate significant bias, helping researchers understand the trade-offs between performance and fairness.

Acceptable Thresholds:

  • Acceptable Fairness: 95-100% fairness score
  • Acceptable Accuracy: 90-100% accuracy score
  • Optimal Zone: Models achieving both acceptable fairness and accuracy
RESEARCH DISCLAIMER:
This bias analysis is conducted for research purposes to advance healthcare equity. Results should be interpreted within the context of the specific test scenarios and should not be the sole basis for model selection in clinical applications.
